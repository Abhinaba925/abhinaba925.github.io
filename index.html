Research

My passion for research transcends specific fields. I believe research is an addiction that can be pursued anytime, anywhere, in any domain. This belief has led me to explore multiple domains, revealing fascinating interconnections through subtle threads.

  • Experimental Study of Free Space Optical Communication System: This project focuses on the applications of multi-input multi-output (MIMO) in beyond 5G systems. The initial phase involved simulation-based studies and design of MIMO and coherent free space optical communication systems to mitigate turbulence-induced fading. The subsequent phase involves experimentally modeling the atmospheric fading channel. We estimated the single-input single-output (SISO) FSO fading channel using an IM/DD experimental setup and maximum-likelihood (ML) estimation technique. For further details, please refer to my recent publication based on this work: IEEE Xplore.
  • Uncertainty in Neural Networks: We propose a modified model of neural network and compared its performance with standard feed-forward neural network (FFNN) and bayesian neural network (BNN). For further details and code you can refer to: Github repo.
